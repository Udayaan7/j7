Inter school football competition
Came second in the inter school football competition

Delhi Public School, Mathura Road organised an Inter-school Football Tournament from 17th February, 2019 to 29th February, 2019 in the school grounds. Teams from many top schools across the city participated in the tournament. The tournament began with a match between the home team and last year's winning team from Amity International School. The Sports teachers Mr. Swaminathan and Miss Kripa Singhal supervised the tournament with the help of a talented team of sports captains and prefects. The prize distribution ceremony for the tournament was held on 29th February, 2019 in the school. The prizes were given away by the School Principal Ratna Shah. The team from Springdales School, Chanakyapuri stood first. The second and third position was grabbed by teams from Carmel Convent and Ramjas School respectively. The winners were applauded by the spectators. All participants received participation certificates and complimentary T-shirts from the sponsor Nike.

Inter School science project competition
Won second price in inter school science project competition

15th of December, 2020 . Our school organized a science exhibition on the 15th of December, 2020. The exhibition was spread over two days and had a number of students and teachers participating and collaborating hand in hand to make the event successful. The science fair was named as "Progress of India'' . This exhibition was open to students from other schools as well as their parents. The schools were given invitation passes to visit. The event started at 10 am and continued till 4 pm. The judging panel for the two days were different. They had to judge the participants that put up their display and give trophies to them. On the 15th of December, 2020 the event was inaugurated by ( give a name of a personality). The event started by the Principal by giving a warm welcome speech and requested the chief guest to assist him in lighting the lamp. The students of class 10 did an opening dance to begin the event post which the students set up their tables. The judges were accompanied by the students from class 11 and 12 and explained all the details of the event. The judges went to each table and meticulously marked them according to their set up. The announcement of the winners was announced the next day after all the judges had submitted their scores. Class 10 came third , they had made charts explaining the Solar power tree in villages. Class 9 achieved the second position and class 11th the first. Class 11th made an apparatus showing how music affects plant growth. The event was a success and all the participants got a medal with a certificate.
